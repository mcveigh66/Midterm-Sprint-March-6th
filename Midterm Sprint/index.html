About us

Moonlight bites is an elegant, dark aesthetic cafe locally owned in the heart of downtown St. John’s.
We offer a quiet atmosphere and specialize in creating dishes that look spooky and taste delicious. Perfect for studying, we are open late everyday!
We believe in using only the freshest ingredients in our food. Coffee made fresh, always.
